About Kanak Bahar

30+ Years of Craftsmanship, Trust & Timeless Jewellery

1996 Year Established
Est. 1996

A Legacy of Craftsmanship
& Trust

Kanak Bahar Jewellers was founded in 1996 by Haradhan Karmakar with a singular vision: to bring the finest handcrafted jewellery to the people of Tripura and beyond. What began as a modest shop in Agartala's Digambar Market has grown into one of the most trusted jewellery destinations in Northeast India.

With over 30 years of expertise, we have served thousands of families through their most precious milestones — weddings, anniversaries, festivals, and everyday moments. Our commitment to certified purity and honest pricing has earned us the trust of customers across generations.

Every piece in our collection — from traditional 22K gold sets to contemporary 24K designs and exquisite silver jewellery — is crafted with the meticulous care that only decades of mastery can bring.
